assets/images/

Place your profile photo here as profile.jpg (or .png), then add to _config.yml:

author:
  avatar: "/assets/images/profile.jpg"

A square image cropped to at least 300×300 px works best. The site will show a grey placeholder circle until a photo is provided.

You can also place a favicon.ico here and enable it via _config.yml:

favicon: "/assets/images/favicon.ico"
